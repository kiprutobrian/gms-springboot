GMS This is a G Suite administration and management app that offers a comprehensive & advanced management and reporting on the G Suite platform, and with a simplified view of the admin controls. The GMS app serves to add custom and advanced functionality to G Suite that many administrators need to better manage, review and remediate the controls, security and eliminate trivial tasks for onboarding and offboarding of users.

GMS Integrations With

The system is integrated with google APIs(Gmail,Drive,Sheet,Admin) for authentication,reporting and alerting/notifications.
